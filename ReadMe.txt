WELCOME TO THE BASIC BUDGET APP WHICH IS DEVELOPED BY USING HTML, CSS AND JAVASCRIPT.

THIS APP IS BASICALLY CALCULATE YOUR MONTHLY INCOME AND EXPENSE


INSTRUCTIONS...


1. + and - symbol represents income and expenses respectively

2. In description box enter your income source or expense you made

3. In value box enter your income or expense amount

4. By clicking check button or by pressing enter key you can add your respective items
